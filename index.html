Stacks & Sliders
official manual

Stacks and Sliders is a game where you both need to be lucky and skilful. Master the art of placing your sliders to build high-value stacks and outscore your opponents across multiple rounds.

7 gold disks and 7 black disks.

black
gold

  1. 1 Shuffle the disks (named sliders) and put them in a neat pile.
  2. 2 Grab the top slider and place it down anywhere on the playing field.
  3. 3 Grab another slider and place it adjacent but not on the previous placed sliders — as if you are playing a game of snake.
  4. 4 Repeat this process until you have placed all sliders.
  5. 5 Repeat all steps with the other player and see who gets the highest score.
